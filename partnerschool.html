Our partner schools

Rysensteen has Partner schools all over the world, each class travels to a partner school one time to live with the students familis. Students from our partner school travels to denmark to live with our studens families. Every class on rysensteen are educated about the contry they are traveling to so the students is able to interact with there hosts and have some knowledge about their culture.
